QR CODES

This is us during our QR Code activity. Our teacher, Sir Alvic let us work in groups for us to be able to do our activity. Our task is to answer questions as we integrate the QR Code in what we do. This activity had given us the chance to use and experience one of the functions of the QR Code not just outside the school where we usually use it. it is great that Sir taught first, he discussed what is QR code and how it can be used in other disciplines and areas as well. On the other hand, it took us some time to grapple its benefit because we are used to a pen-and-paper type of assessment.

-- Alecson Cidro & Janina Dane Aquino(Authors)
