Wordcloud

A word cloud is an effective tool for visualizing what the audience truly believes about a topic. There are numerous web-based word cloud-creation tools available for this type of activity, but I prefer to create word clouds manually using PowerPoint. With these, I learned that patience, creativity, dedication, and editing knowledge are all required to create a creative and successful word cloud. Through these activities, I discovered that there are numerous benefits and challenges that Information, Communication, and Technology can provide us in terms of learning and teaching.
