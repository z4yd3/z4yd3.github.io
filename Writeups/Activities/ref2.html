Expectations and Insight about the course TTL1/ADTECH

I expect that in our course, Technology for Teaching and Learning 1, I will be learning a lot from the course itself and from the teacher. I know that this course will be a challenging one because we'll surely be struggling and face different trials as it's our first time to encounter this course. Also, I expected that this course will help me to learn more things about Technology. I expect that this course will serve as an opportunity for me to explore the broad term/ topic. Not only that I'll get to know more concepts about the topics to be discussed, but most specially to get the real essence of having this course in our program which is to help us become prepared and equipped with a firm knowledge in our future professions - teachers. In addition to, I'll expect that this course will help me to become informative, innovative, and creative person in all aspects of life as I apply what I have learned about Technology.

There are a lot things I would like to learn in this course. Aside from the different concept of technology, I would like to learn how to become innovative and creative when it comes to coming up with the different effective strategies to be applied in teaching as my future profession. I like to expand my knowledge on the different effective tools to be used in building a connection and relationship towards my students as I teach them about this course someday. I like to know the real importance of studying this course and how it the presence as well as the absence of technology affects the teaching and learning process.
